Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

SpotSave: Car Locator ("SpotSave", "we", "our", or "us") is built by Websage Inc. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use the SpotSave iOS app.

Information We Collect

SpotSave is designed with privacy first. We collect only what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Location data — Your GPS coordinates are used to save your parking spot and help you navigate back to your car. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Camera — The camera is used only for the AR compass feature to help point you toward your car. We do not capture, store, or transmit any camera images or video.

Data Storage

All data (parking spots, history) is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal or location data. We have no access to your parking history.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties. Period.

In-App Purchases

SpotSave offers an optional Pro subscription ($2.99/month) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Apple.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The only analytics we receive are the aggregate, anonymized statistics provided by Apple through App Store Connect (downloads, crashes, usage), which contain no personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

SpotSave is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because all data stays on-device and no accounts are required, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

Your Rights

To delete all SpotSave data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, this permanently removes everything.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify users through an app update.
