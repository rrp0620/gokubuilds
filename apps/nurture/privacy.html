Privacy Policy

Nurture: Baby Feed Tracker  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Nurture: Baby Feed Tracker ("the App") is developed by Websage Inc. We are committed to protecting the privacy of your family's data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

The App collects only the data necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name, used for authentication via Supabase Auth.
  • Baby Data: Baby name, birthdate, and optional photo — stored securely in our database and accessible only to you and caregivers you explicitly invite.
  • Tracking Logs: Feed sessions (time, duration, type, side), sleep sessions, diaper logs, and weight entries you manually create.
  • Device Data: Standard app usage data for crash reporting. No advertising identifiers.

We do not collect location data, contacts, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

How We Use Your Data

  • To store and sync your tracking logs across your devices and with invited caregivers
  • To generate insights and predictions based on your logged data
  • To provide partner sharing via invite codes

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Row-level security ensures only you and caregivers you invite can access your baby's data.

Partner Sharing

When you generate an invite code and share it with a caregiver, that person gains access to your baby's tracking data. You can revoke access at any time from the Settings screen.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for use by parents and caregivers (adults). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Baby data entered by parents is treated as the parent's data.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app.
