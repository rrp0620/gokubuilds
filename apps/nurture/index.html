Questions

Real questions from
real tired parents

How quickly can I log a feed at 3am when I can barely see? +
Under 5 seconds. Open the app, tap the big 🍼 button, and you're done. The app timestamps it automatically. We designed every single interaction for sleep-deprived, one-handed, zero-brain-capacity moments.
Do both parents need a subscription? +
Nope. One Pro or Max subscription covers your whole family — both parents share access with one subscription. Just share a 6-character invite code and your partner is synced in seconds.
Is this safe for tracking a newborn? How accurate are the insights? +
Nurture: Baby Tracker is a logging and tracking tool, not a medical device. Our insights are based on AAP guidelines and reviewed by pediatric consultants. Always consult your pediatrician for medical decisions — we make that easy by generating reports you can share directly with them.
What if I forget to log something? +
You can backfill entries at any time. Tap the + button, set the time manually, and add it. Forgot this morning's 2am feed? No problem — add it now and everything recalculates correctly. We don't shame you for being human.
Does it work for formula, breastfeeding, and mixed feeding? +
Yes — all three, and you can switch between them anytime. Log breast in ounces or minutes per side. Log formula by volume. Log combination feeding with separate entries. No judgment, no pressure, just tracking.
