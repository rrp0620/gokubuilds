The Opportunity
Africa's Luxury Hospitality Market Is Underserved — and Growing Fast

East Africa's luxury travel market is expanding at 15% year-over-year, yet high-quality lodge supply remains critically scarce. Tanzania — home to the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, and Zanzibar — is the continent's premier safari destination, attracting over $2 billion in annual tourism revenue. A rising wave of Indian HNIs and diaspora investors are seeking real-asset exposure to Africa's growth story, but lack an institutional-grade vehicle to participate. MHV was built to fill that gap.
