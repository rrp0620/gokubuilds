About the Fund

Development-Led Hospitality. Done Right.

Maendeleo — meaning "development" in Swahili — defines both our name and our philosophy. We don't just build lodges; we build economies. Every property we develop creates jobs, uplifts communities, and unlocks the full potential of East Africa's most extraordinary landscapes.

The MHV Fund offers investors a single, professionally managed vehicle to access a diversified portfolio of hospitality assets across Tanzania. Rather than managing individual project risk, investors participate in the performance of the fund as a whole.

Our team has direct operational experience building and running camps in Tanzania — this isn't a thesis, it's a track record. Mapito is proof.

Investment Approach

Why East Africa. Why Now.

The African hospitality market remains dramatically underserved relative to global demand. Rising international arrivals, a growing Indian and Asian investor base, and a scarcity of quality supply create a rare entry point for disciplined capital.

Operational Expertise

We don't underwrite projections — we've lived them. Our principals have built and operated camps on the ground in Tanzania. We understand the permits, the partners, and the pitfalls most funds miss.

Supply Scarcity

Quality lodge inventory in prime locations remains scarce. New development is constrained by land access, licensing, and operational know-how — exactly where we have an edge. Scarcity drives premium valuations.

Community Flywheel

Lodges that lift the communities around them generate stronger guest loyalty, better staff retention, and regulatory goodwill. Our community development model isn't charity — it's competitive advantage.

Diversified Portfolio

A single-fund structure pools capital across multiple assets, spreading operational risk while maintaining the return profile of direct property exposure. Investors gain diversification without the complexity.

Credible Structure

The fund is being structured with full legal and regulatory rigour in Tanzania. Investor protections, reporting standards, and governance are built in from day one — not bolted on after the fact.

Target Markets

Our primary distribution focus is India, where appetite for alternative, real-asset investments with fixed-income characteristics is significant and growing. Secondary access in the USA and UK.

Community Impact

When We Develop a Lodge, Thousands Benefit.

Every MHV property is engineered to uplift the community it operates within. Not as a branding exercise — as a genuine operating model. Local employment, supplier partnerships, and infrastructure contributions are built into every development plan.

"When we develop a lodge, it uplifts thousands in the area."

One of our flagship community partnerships is with Hope for Girls, a center providing education and support to young women in Tanzania. Our partners have donated a full year's food supply to the center — and we are currently building them a brand new facility.

This is the kind of impact that cannot be replicated by funds chasing generic Serengeti exposure. It's built on relationships, presence, and years of doing the work on the ground.
