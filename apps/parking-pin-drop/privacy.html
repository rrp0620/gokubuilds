Privacy Policy

SpotSave: Car Locator  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

SpotSave: Car Locator ("the App") is developed by Websage Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

The App collects only the data necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Location Data: The App accesses your device's GPS location to save your parking spot and display it on a map. Location data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Contacts, photos, or any other device data

How We Use Your Data

Location data is used solely to:

  • Save your parking spot coordinates
  • Display your saved spot on the in-app map
  • Provide walking directions back to your car

All data processing occurs entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone.

Data Storage

All saved locations are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. We have no access to this data. Deleting the App removes all stored data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple Maps for map display and navigation. Apple's privacy policy applies to map services: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
